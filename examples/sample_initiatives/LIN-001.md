# Self-serve onboarding wizard

Customers consistently drop off at signup because they have to email us for a demo before they can try the product. Sales is the bottleneck for low-ACV accounts who would otherwise self-qualify and convert in minutes.

A three-step wizard at first login should let a new workspace owner pick a use case, invite teammates, and seed a sample dataset without any human in the loop. Long term this also unblocks paid acquisition channels because the trial experience finally stands on its own.

Activation rate from signup to first meaningful action is the headline metric. We expect at least a 20% lift in week-one activation within two release cycles of shipping the wizard.
