# Billing usage dashboard for admins

Workspace admins on metered plans currently have no in-product way to see how their usage is trending against their cap until they get a "you're at 90%" email from billing. That email is the first time they realize they are about to overage, and it shows up too late to do anything about it.

We need an in-product dashboard that visualizes current-period consumption, projects end-of-period spend based on rolling trend, and lets admins set their own soft alerts at 50% / 75% / 90% thresholds. This is the same surface our enterprise champions ask for in every renewal conversation.

Done well, this reduces inbound "what is this charge" support tickets and gives admins enough lead time to upgrade proactively. We treat this as a retention play, not a billing tweak.
